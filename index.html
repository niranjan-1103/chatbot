🧩 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding Technique

When anxiety takes over, focus on your immediate surroundings to bring yourself back to the present moment:

  • 5 things you can see around you.
  • 4 things you can touch or feel.
  • 3 things you can hear (birds, traffic, music).
  • 2 things you can smell (coffee, grass, soap).
  • 1 thing you can taste or a positive thing to say to yourself.

💡 Immediate Stress-Relief Tips

💧 Hydrate First Drink a glass of cold water slowly. It forces breathing regulation.
🚶 Take a Step Away Stand up, stretch, or step outside for 2 minutes of fresh air.
✍️ Brain Dump Write down everything worrying you on a piece of paper, then throw it away.
🎵 Lo-Fi Beats Put on soft music without lyrics to help focus your brain.

About Aura

Your Calm Space

Aura is designed to help you process stress, anxiety, and the daily pressures of life. It uses generative AI to provide active listening, empathy, and evidence-based self-care tools.

⚠️ IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER

Aura is an AI-powered conversational agent built to support emotional well-being. It is NOT a medical device, a human therapist, or a clinical replacement. Aura cannot diagnose conditions, prescribe medications, or provide clinical treatments. By using this application, you acknowledge that this is a supportive guidance tool and not a clinical intervention.

If you are experiencing persistent mental health challenges or thoughts of self-harm, please seek professional support from a licensed therapist, counselor, or medical doctor.

🔒 Privacy & Confidentiality

We care deeply about your privacy. Conversations are processed securely in real-time to generate responses and are not stored permanently by Aura. Your voice recordings are processed locally in your browser and are never sent to our servers.
